### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How accurate is Telepathy?**
A: Our models achieve >85% accuracy across all emotions, validated on industry-standard datasets (RAVDESS, CREMA-D). Enterprise customers can train custom models for even higher accuracy.

**Q: What languages do you support?**
A: Currently English, with Spanish and Mandarin in beta. We're adding new languages monthly based on customer demand.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, never store audio longer than necessary, and are SOC 2 Type II and HIPAA compliant.

**Q: How long does analysis take?**
A: Real-time predictions take <500ms. Batch processing depends on file size but typically completes within seconds.

**Q: Can I integrate with my existing tools?**
A: Yes! We offer REST APIs, webhooks, and pre-built integrations with Zoom, Teams, Twilio, and more.

**Q: What emotions can you detect?**
A: Standard: neutral, happy, sad, angry, fearful, surprised, disgusted, calm. Enterprise customers can add custom emotions.
